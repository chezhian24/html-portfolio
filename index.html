CHEZHIAN

WEB DEVOLPMENT

The name Chezhian is of Tamil origin and is commonly used as a given name for boys.
It is derived from the Tamil language, where "chezhian" (செழியன்)
can be broken down into two parts:
"chezhi" (செழி) meaning "artistic" or "skillful," and "an" (அன்)
which is a suffix used to indicate masculinity
or ownership
